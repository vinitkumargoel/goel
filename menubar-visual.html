Menu-bar dropdown — 10 concepts

A compact status-bar popover so you can drive downloads without opening the main window. Every concept below satisfies the three must-haves — and each pairs with a different status-item icon treatment (shown as the little tile above each popover). Real menu-bar popovers are narrow (~270–340 px); these are drawn at true size. Toggle Light/Dark to check both.
